Upcoming Event - Purdue Cup

Purdue cup is one of the biggest Indonesian sport tournament in MidWest. There are usually basketball, futsal, badminton and volleyball tournaments. However, this year we are also going to have 3 vs 3 basketball woman.

This event is not only about competition, but also a solidarity and friendship.
